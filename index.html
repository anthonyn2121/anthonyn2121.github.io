Projects

I pride myself in the projects I do, here is a quick summary of my favorite ones. If you would like more detail on any projects or check out my other projects, check out my projects page. I plan to do a lot more projects and this page will be constantly updated

Simulation of chaotic motion for n-link pendulum

Here I derive the differential equation of an n-linked pendulum and numerically simulate the position of each joint

RRT motion planning for 6DOF manipulator

I implement the rapidly-exploring random tree (RRT) motion planning algorithm and visualize it with a simulation

Vector Field motion planning for 6DOF manipulator

I also tried my hand in implementing a potential field planner on a robotic arm.

A multi-omnidirectional robot

As a part of my mechatronics course, we were tasked to create a battle bot as our final project. Here I worked with a team of 3 to create a holonomic robot intended to do devastating damage to other robots
